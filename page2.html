Learn About Artificial Intelligence

Understand how AI works, how it is trained, and have fun learning!

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that creates machines capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include:

  • Understanding natural language
  • Recognizing images and objects
  • Solving complex problems
  • Making decisions

How AIs are Trained

AIs are trained using data. The process usually involves:

  • Data Collection: Gathering large datasets relevant to the task.
  • Preprocessing: Cleaning and organizing data for training.
  • Model Training: Using algorithms (like neural networks) to learn patterns from the data.
  • Evaluation: Testing the AI on new data to measure performance.
  • Iteration: Repeating the process to improve accuracy.

Types of AI

AI can be categorized into:

  • Narrow AI: Performs specific tasks (like image recognition or chatbots).
  • General AI: Can perform any intellectual task humans can do (still in research phase).
  • Machine Learning: AI that learns from data.
  • Deep Learning: Uses neural networks with many layers for complex pattern recognition.

Real-World Applications

AI is used everywhere today, including:

  • Virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa
  • Recommendation systems like YouTube and Netflix
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Healthcare diagnosis and research
  • Fraud detection in banking
