How To Use AI

1. Be Specific

Instead of saying "help with homework", say "explain Newton's laws in simple terms".

2. Give Context

Tell the AI what you are doing. Example: "I am writing a school essay about climate change."

3. Ask For Formats

You can request lists, summaries, or step-by-step instructions.

4. Improve Your Prompt

If the answer isn't good, ask again with more details.

5. Example Prompt

Bad: write essay

Good: Write a 300 word essay explaining how solar energy works for a middle school student.
